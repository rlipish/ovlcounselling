Authenticity, Connection, Compassion

Life can become overwhelming and you can feel “stuck” with anxiety, depression, interpersonal difficulties, parenting challenges, or life transitions. This may negatively impact your daily functioning.

I provide a non-judgmental and empathic space where you can process your thoughts and feelings, gain insight, and take actions to get “unstuck”.

In examining your patterns of thoughts, emotions, and behaviours, together we will explore and utilize interventions informed by Attachment Theory, Family Systems Theory, Cognitive Behaviour Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT), and Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT).

Through this process, we can explore your strengths and abilities, as well as discover what may be holding you back from living a meaningful life congruent with your values.

My Background

I am a Registered Social Worker based in Calgary, Alberta. I have completed a Bachelor of Science in Psychology (University of Calgary), a Bachelor of Social Work (University of Victoria) and a Master of Social Work specializing in clinical social work practice (University of Calgary).

With 10 years of experience working with children, youth and families, I have worked with individuals from diverse backgrounds and intersecting identities in their homes and in the community.
