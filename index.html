INTRODUCTION

Life can become overwhelming and you may feel “stuck” with anxiety, depression, interpersonal difficulties, parenting challenges, or life transitions.

I provide a non-judgmental and empathic space where you can process your thoughts and feelings, gain insight, and take actions to get “unstuck”. In examining your patterns of thoughts, emotions, and behaviours, together we can explore your strengths and abilities, as well as what is holding you back from living a meaningful life congruent with your values.

Ory Li Pi Shan, MSW, RSW

I am a Registered Social Worker with a Master of Social Work degree specializing in clinical social work practice based in Calgary, Alberta.

I have over 10 years of experience working with children, youth and families. I have worked with individuals from diverse backgrounds and intersecting identities in their homes and in the community.

My therapeutic style draws from Attachment Theory, Family Therapy, Cognitive Behaviour Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT), and Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT).

I value authenticity, connection, and compassion.

SERVICES OFFERED

I help individuals struggling with:

  • Anxiety
  • Depression
  • Interpersonal Difficulties
  • Life Transitions
  • Parenting Challenges

I believe a good therapeutic alliance is essential to collaborating with you in achieving your therapeutic goals.

If you are ready to take action and make a commitment to your values, contact me for a free 15-minute consultation to see if we can do our best work together.
